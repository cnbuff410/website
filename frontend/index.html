About Me

Most of my days are spent on Android, iOS and AppEngine. My favorite programming language is Go and Objective-C. I also write(or used to write) tons of C, Java and Python code.

I was a PhD student in CUBoulder. During that time I was constantly hacking on random stuff and have created several designs, proof-of-concept implementations, and published a few papers.

I spent three summers working at Google Research, Android, and Google Ads team, during which I am very fortunate to have the opportunity to work with many world-class programmers/researchers. I did the very first prototype version of the Official Gesture Keyboard, which is then introduced in Android 4.2 system.
